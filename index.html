Fabric Python
Minecraft Enhancement Client Mod for AI Research
 

Fabric Python is a client mod that provides various enhancement for the purpose of AI research.

It should not be used in a multiplayer server. Admis may very likely ban you since a number of features can cause unfairness to other players.

We now discuss its features in detail.  
 
Xray
 

Xray makes certain types of blocks transparent. This may faciliate finding valuable ores (such as ancient debris and diamond ores) or dungeons. To start or exit the Xray mode, enter in the chat:

/xray 

By default, Xray applies to the following blocks: netherrack, stone, granite, diorite, and andesite. More types can be added or deleted by the following commands:

/xray add [Namespace ID] 

/xray del [Namespace ID] 

When Xray is on, near the players there will be green and red cross patterns (this part of the code is from shedaniel's light overlay. The green area means that the players can safely step in. The red area means that the player may fall.  

 
Night vision
 

Night vision lets the render to apply light to all blocks. It can be used for one-side mining (in which we don't place torchs) or for Xray. To turn on or off night vision, enter in the chat:

/nightvision 
 
Jump boost
 

Jumpboost can improve the general gaming experience, especially allowing the players to cross walls (the collision box of which is 1.5-block high). To turn on or off jump boost, enter in the chat:

/jumpboost 
 
Leviation
 

Leviation can make the player move higher and higher. To turn on or off leviation, enter in the chat:

/leviation 
 
Autoattack
 

Autoattack will attack nearby monsters (except endermen and zombified piglins) when the player is holding a sword. To turn on or off autoattack, enter in the chat:

/autoattack 
 
Safe falling
 

Safe falling waivers the falling damage. This feature is by default turned on. To turn off or on this feature, enter in the chat:

/safefall 
 
Autobuild
 

Autobuild horizontally extends the current floor or ceil. When the player holds the blocks in the main hand, and there are more than three of such blocks near the player, it will start to extend this floor or ceil if it is within the reach. To turn off or on this feature, enter in the chat:

/autobuild 

To restrict the range of autobuild, which would help more accurate autobuild, set a bounding box by specifying the two edge points:

/pos1 [x1] [y1] [z1]  

/pos2 [x2] [y2] [z2]  
 
Autofeed cows and pigs
 

Autofeed allows automatically feeding cows and pigs if the player is holding the food items. To turn off or on this feature, enter in the chat:

/autofeedpig 

/autofeedcow 
 
Smart sneaking
 

Smart sneaking prevents the player from falling. It is smart in that it does not slow down the player. To turn on or off smart sneaking, enter in the chat:

/sneaking 
 
Easyrow
 

Easyrow simplifies the rowing by setting the boat to align with the player's focus. This feature is by default turned on. To turn on or off easy row, enter in the chat:

/easyrow 
 
Monster glowing
 

Monster glowing shows the outline of the monsters. To turn on or off monster glowing, enter in the chat:

/mobglow 
 
Safemine
 

Safemine prevents the player from accidentally opening a cave or other dangerous open areas. To turn on or off safemine, enter in the chat:

/safemine 
 
Autotorch 
 

Autotorch automatically places torchs when the place has low block lighting if the player is holding torchs on the main hand or the off hand. To turn on or off autotorch, enter in the chat:

/autotorch 
 
Autolantern 
 

Autolantern automatically places lanterns on the ceil when the place has low block lighting if the player is holding lanterns on the main hand. This is specially designed to make the ceil not too dark. To turn on or off autolantern, enter in the chat:

/autolantern 
 
Study mode 
 

Study mode locks the player for a specified length of time. This allows players to be away from the keyboard and study. To start the study mode, enter in the chat:

/study [minutes to study] 

To exit the study mode, the players should wait for that length of time. Or, the player can also exit and relaunch the game.
 
Python support, broadly 
 

Fabric Python provides an API to Python, which allows Python to control the player's actions. More introduction would be provided in the near future.
